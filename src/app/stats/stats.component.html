Stat Clarifications

Season: Which season the stats are from.

Games: Total number of games played during the season.

Goals: Goals scored by the player.

Assists: Amount of assists on scored team goals.

Points: The added number of goals and assists adds up to total points.

+/-: A balancing scale between being on the ice for goals scored for your team(+) and against your team(-).

PIM: A total of penalty minutes served by the player.

TOI: Total time on ice per game, meaning the average amount of time played by the player each game.
